Smart Plant Monitoring

Aligarh Muslim University's non-native plant collection is now part of our smart city initiative. Each plant is monitored with IoT sensors that track soil moisture, health metrics, and environmental conditions in real-time. This digital twin system helps maintain optimal growing conditions while conserving water through smart irrigation.

The monitoring system collects data on soil moisture, nutrient levels, temperature, and environmental stressors to ensure the exotic plants continue to thrive in Aligarh's climate. Authorized personnel can remotely control irrigation systems and receive alerts when conditions require attention.

Explore each plant's real-time health indicators below, or view the campus-wide plant health map to identify areas requiring maintenance or special care.
